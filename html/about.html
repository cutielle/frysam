Love Ignites Success

Fry Samaritans Food Station found joy in providing budget-friendly yet delicious meals to students, centering Pampanga State Agricultural University. From 2019 until now, despite initial hesitations we're fueled by our passion for cooking; we took the leap to become pioneers in introducing this savory delight to Magalang, Pampanga. We ride on a humble venture, starting with a simple fried noodles to meals that can power academic pursuits with a hearty satisfaction.
